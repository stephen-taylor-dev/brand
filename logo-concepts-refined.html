Spark / Eureka / Navigator

Deeper variations on the three strongest themes — kept simple, bold, and catchy. Each one should work from favicon to billboard.

Spark Theme

Energy, brilliance, a flash of intelligence

taylorsmart.ai
S1
The Pulse
Tapered blades with sparkle accents
Sharper, more refined take on the spark. Tapered blade shapes radiate from a cyan core — feels like a camera flash or a pulse of energy. Small diagonal accents add a sparkle quality. More dynamic than the original.
taylorsmart.ai
S2
The North Star
Asymmetric star — guiding upward
The top point stretches higher, pulling the eye upward. Suggests guidance, aspiration, and direction. Like a north star leading the way. Combines spark energy with navigator-like purpose. Subtle shift from center adds dynamism.
taylorsmart.ai
S3
The Core
Inverted spark — intelligence revealed
The spark shape is filled with cyan and framed by a white ring. Inverts the figure/ground — the intelligence IS the shape, contained within structure. Feels like peering into a reactor core or power source. Bold, distinctive, heavy.

Eureka Theme

The moment of insight, illumination, bright ideas

taylorsmart.ai
E1
The Beacon
Clean lightbulb with spark filament
The original Eureka refined — cleaner proportions, the spark mark living inside as the filament. The bulb is a bold white circle, the base is minimal bands. Radiating lines add energy. The spark-inside-the-bulb creates a logo-within-a-logo.
taylorsmart.ai
E2
The Filament
Raw energy — no shell, just the flame
Strip the lightbulb away — what's left is the raw energy itself. A flowing, organic cyan flame shape that feels alive and dynamic. More expressive and artistic than the geometric concepts. Suggests pure creative energy and innovation without boundaries.
taylorsmart.ai
E3
The Moment
The "aha" — a face having an idea
The most playful concept: a simple circular face with a spark above its head — the universal "lightbulb moment." Friendly, approachable, and instantly understood by any audience. Great mascot potential. The spark above could animate as a thinking/processing indicator.

Navigator Theme

Finding the smart path, solving complexity

taylorsmart.ai
N1
The Route
Just the solution — no noise
Strip the maze away entirely. All that remains is the confident, clean path from problem (bottom-left) to solution (top-right). A grid of faint dots hints at hidden complexity, but the bold cyan route cuts through it decisively. Minimal, impactful, tells the whole story in one line.
taylorsmart.ai
N2
The Circuit Path
PCB trace aesthetic — tech DNA
The route rendered as a circuit board trace with square junction nodes at every turn. Faint unused traces in the background show roads not taken. Bridges navigator pathfinding with deep tech identity. Feels engineered, precise, intentional — every turn is calculated.
taylorsmart.ai
N3
The Spiral
Angular spiral converging to the answer
A square spiral path that winds inward to a glowing cyan center — the solution. The angular geometry keeps it feeling technical rather than organic. Communicates drilling down through layers of complexity to reach the core answer. The journey is visible, the destination is certain.
