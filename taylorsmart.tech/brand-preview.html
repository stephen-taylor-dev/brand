05 — Brand Voice

Voice & Tone

How taylorsmart.tech sounds in every interaction — confident, clear, and human.

⚡

Confident

We know our craft. Direct statements, no hedging. "We build it" not "We can try to help."

⚖

Clear

No jargon unless necessary. Your neighbor should understand it.

★

Smart

Show expertise through insight, not buzzwords. Demonstrate value with specifics.

♥

Local

We're your neighbors. Approachable, respectful, genuinely invested in the community.

Messaging Examples

Do

  • "Smart solutions, Taylored for you."
  • "Smart tech. Full service."
  • "Ready to build something smart?"
  • "Free consultation. No jargon. Just a conversation about what technology can do for your business."

Don't

  • "We leverage synergistic paradigms to drive outcomes."
  • "Our innovative solutions may help your business."
  • "We're the best in the business!!!"
  • "Contact us to learn more about our services."
06 — Usage Guidelines

Logo Rules

Protect the integrity of the mark across all applications.

Do

  • Use on dark navy or white backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space equal to icon height
  • Use provided SVG files for pixel-perfect rendering
  • Scale proportionally
  • Use monochrome version when color unavailable

Don't

  • Rotate, skew, or distort the logo
  • Add drop shadows, glows, or 3D effects
  • Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Separate ".tech" from "taylorsmart" in the wordmark
  • Change the weight contrast in the name treatment
