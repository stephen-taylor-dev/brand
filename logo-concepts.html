CONCEPT A

The Sage

Owl — Wisdom that sees what others can't

The owl is the universal symbol of wisdom and intelligence. This geometric owl face is bold and immediately recognizable — the large cyan eyes suggest AI-powered vision, always watching, always processing. The angular ear tufts give it a sharp, alert quality. Owls see in the dark — taylorsmart.ai sees solutions where others see only problems.

Strong mascot potential. Works as a favicon (the eyes are distinctive even at 16px). Has personality and warmth while still feeling technical.

CONCEPT B

The Clever

Fox — Agile, cunning, always one step ahead

The fox is the embodiment of cleverness — quick thinking, resourceful, elegant in its approach. This geometric fox face uses sharp angular shapes with cyan inner ears, creating a striking, modern mark. The dark "eye mask" markings give it a distinctive, almost superhero-like quality.

Foxes represent finding the smartest path, not the obvious one — perfect for a company that solves complicated business problems with technology. Strong visual identity with great mascot and merchandise potential.

The Navigator

Maze — Finding the smartest path through complexity

This concept directly visualizes the core value proposition: you have a complicated problem, we find the smart path through it. The maze could be animated on the website — the path "solving" itself as the page loads.

A bold, geometric horse head silhouette with a glowing cyan eye. The knight metaphor is powerful: when your business faces obstacles, taylorsmart.ai doesn't go around them — we leap over them. Strong, authoritative, and immediately communicates strategic intelligence.

The Spark

4-Pointed Star — Pure energy and precision

The original v2 concept included here for comparison. A bold four-pointed geometric star with a glowing cyan intelligence core. Clean, abstract, highly geometric. Represents energy, precision, and a flash of brilliance. Circuit traces in the background ground it in technology.
